the alien and the wild west

Game description

In 2030 an UFO came to the Earth and crashed in the desert. Try to survive in the Wild West among wild bees, plants and slugs. Help the Alien find spares for its broken space ship and reach it in the end of the map, so that the Alien can get out of here!
